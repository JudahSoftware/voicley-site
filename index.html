Speak. Organize. Listen.

Three moves no other app chains together — the way ideas actually move from your head to something you can use.

🎙

Capture

Just talk. Say “new note” and keep going — each thought lands in its own place automatically, punctuated and clean. It never drops a word, so you can think as fast as you speak.

🗂

Organize

Later, your ideas are all there — separate, titled, waiting. Drag them into folders for a project, a goal, a strategy. Organize your thinking like a mind, not a filing cabinet.

🎧

Listen

Then hit play and hear your ideas back — a whole folder, in order, in your own words. Review your strategy on a drive. Relive last week’s thinking on a walk. Hands-free.

The part no one else does

Listen to your own mind, strategically.

Every app can record you. Voicley plays you back. Queue a folder of goals and strategies into a listening program, set the order, and press play — your best thinking becomes a private podcast of your own ideas, ready whenever you need to get back into it.

Everything a voice deserves

Built around one law: capture first. Your words hit disk from the first syllable — the network, the engine, even the app can hiccup and you still lose nothing.

⚡

Live as you talk

Words appear in well under a second, punctuated and clean — a streaming engine built for real time, so your thoughts keep pace with your mouth.

🗣

Your voice runs it

“New note.” “New task… end task.” “New paragraph.” “Stop dictation.” The whole app answers to your voice — the buttons are just backup.

🎧

Read back to you

Every note reads itself aloud; every recording replays. Line up folders into a listening program and relive your thinking, hands-free.

📋

Notes, tasks & meetings

Say “new task” mid-thought and it files itself to your task list. Long recordings become meetings, broken out by speaker.NEW

📁

Organize & sync

Drag ideas into folders and sub-folders; everything — text and audio — syncs across your phone and PC, in the order you set.

📓

OneNote, live

Dictation can stream straight into your OneNote pages as you speak — titles, sections and all — so your archive fills itself.

🔒

Local-first

Works fully offline. Your recordings and text live on your device first — the cloud is a convenience, not a requirement.

🛟

Never loses a word

Audio is saved from the first frame. Dead network, a crash, a phone call mid-sentence — it recovers and re-transcribes. If you said it, it’s saved.

The capture-first promise. An app that drops your words is worse than useless — you stop trusting your own notes, and then you stop using it. Voicley writes audio to disk from the first frame, survives dead networks, mid-session crashes and phone calls, and re-transcribes anything that didn’t finish. If you said it, it’s saved.
